## PROSPECTUS CUM INFORMATION BULLETIN

Lateral Entry Admission in Diploma Level Technical Institutes under the Dept. of Higher Education for of ITI Pass-outs

Academic Year 2021-2022

Central Selection Committee-2021 Education (Higher) Department Govt. of Tripura

## Lateral Entry Admission for ITI Pass-outs in Diploma Engineering Programmes for Academic Session 2021-22 Under the Dept. of Higher Education, Govt. of Tripura.

Opening Date: 27.08.2021

Closing Date: 10.09.2021

The  ITI  Pass  -  out  students  from  NCVT  approved  Institutions  will  get  the  opportunity  for admission  in  the  3 rd Semester  of    Diploma  Engineering  Programmes  for  the  very  first  time  in various Government Polytechnics of Tripura in the academic session 2021- 22. The admission process  will  be  done  through  online  mode  only.  The  candidates  have  to  apply  in  the  specific website  for  Lateral  Entry  Admission  in  Diploma  Level  Institutes  under  the  Dept.  of  Higher Education, Govt. of Tripura. There is no printed application form available for the purpose.

## Guidelines for admission in Diploma Engineering through Lateral Entry Scheme from ITI

- A. Candidates should be resident of Tripura and should have valid PRTC.
- B. Candidate with  Madhyamik or 10 th standard passed with 2  years NCVT approved ITI courses (as per Table -1) will be eligible for taking admission into 2 nd year (3 rd semester) of Diploma Engineering through Lateral Entry Scheme
- C. Intake capacity shall be the unfilled vacancies in the first year plus 10% of the 'Approved Intake' which shall be over and above i.e. supernumerary to the 'Approved Intake'.
- D. There is no upper age limit for candidates for admission into Diploma Engineering.
- E. As per AICTE, candidates should have secured 35% marks in aggregate in Madhyamik or 10 th standard or equivalent examination from TBSE/CBSE or any other TBSE recognized boards.
- F. Candidate shall produce all original documents including character certificate issued by Head of  the  Institute  last  attended  or  any  Gazetted  Officer.  Such  character  certificates must be issued within six month before the date of document verification and admission.
- G. Reservation of unfilled vacancies shall be as per the Roster of seats of the preceding Diploma Admission and for additional 10% supernumerary seats new roster will be prepared as per the guidelines of state government.
- H. Mapping of  ITI  trades  with  corresponding  Diploma  Engineering  Programmes  shall  be  as  in Table -1 below:

- I. Registration  and  application  should  be  done  through  online  portal  of  DEEET  maintained  by CSC.
- J. Application  fee  of Rs.  350 /-  (for  all  candidate  other  than  BPL)  and Rs.  200 /-  (for  BPL candidates) must be paid by the candidates through online mode.
- K. Beyond  the  examination  fee,  the  payment  gateway  service  charges  also  would  be  borne  by candidate themselves.
- L. Merit list  will  be  prepared  on  the  basis  of  total  marks  obtained  in  ITI  final  examinations  as reflected in Consolidated Statement of marks.

- M. Counselling will be done through online. Prior to online counselling candidates have to feed their choices of Branch and Institute in the portal. After that, online portal will automatically allot  a  seat  to  respective  candidate  and  an  allotment  letter  will  be  generated  which  will  be available in the portal for download and print. Students from any ITI trade will be allowed to take admission in corresponding Diploma Engineering Programme only as stated in Table-1 above.
- N. Reservation of seats will be as per norms set by Govt. of Tripura.
- O. Candidate must be physically present for producing all original documents at the time of taking admission in respective polytechnics. Candidates also have to fulfil all formalities of respective Institutes.
- P. After completion of 1 st round of counselling, there may be 2 nd round and Spot counselling if required based on availability of seats, which will be done within the permitted time-frame of admission process as per AICTE directives.
- Q. During Online Application, candidate has to upload current colour Photograph, full Signature and other documents, otherwise candidate will lose his/her candidature/claim of admission for the year. Detail list of documents are available in the prospectus.
- R. First round document verification will be done before publication of merit list. If documents of any  candidate  are  found  inappropriate/incorrect,  in  that  case  candidate  will  lose  his/her candidature.
- S. BPL  candidates  seeking  relaxation  of  fees  must  provide  their  unique  Ration  Card  Number during  online  application  process.  After  verification,  if  the  claim  of  any  candidate  as  'BPL category' is found false/fake, then that candidate must pay full fees of Rs. 350/-, failing which, candidate will lose his / her candidature /claim of admission for the year.
- T. Candidates must produce hard copy of their 'online application Acknowledgement page' and photocopy  of all relevant documents  along  with original  documents  during  document verification  and  admission.  Without  verification  of  original  documents,  no  candidate  will  be allowed to take admission. If documents of any candidate are found inappropriate/incorrect, in that case candidate will lose his/her candidature for taking admission.

## Reservation of Seats:

Reservation  of  seats  for  SC  /  ST  /  Person  with  Disability  (PwD)/  Wards  of  Ex-serviceman  (ESM) candidates  shall  be  as  per  the  Reservation  Policy  of  the  Government  of  Tripura.  In  case  of  PwD candidates, the percentage of disability and type of disability will be as per Government norms. The Certificate  (s)  issued  by  the  appropriate  authority  (Dept.  of  Sainik  Welfare  for  ESM  and  Dept.  of Social  Welfare  for  PwD  candidates)  shall  have  to  be  produced  in  original  at  the  time  of  document verification.

In case of Wards of Ex-Serviceman (ESM) , a candidate should get his/her Online Acknowledgement  Page endorsed  from  Dept.  of  Sainik  Welfare  and  the  candidate  must  fulfill  all other conditions set by CSC - 2021. Such candidates mandatorily have to produce the duly endorsed Acknowledgement Page at the time of document verification.

However, if any amendment /change is/ are made after issue of this Notification, the same shall be notified in local newspaper and also in official website.

The candidates are required to go through the instructions carefully before submitting online application.

Once an application is received, it will be considered that the candidate agrees to all terms &amp;  conditions,  rules  &amp;  regulations  stipulated  in  the  Information  Bulletin  and  notices published by Central Selection Committee (CSC).

Any application not in compliance with the conditions specified by the CSC 2021 is liable to be rejected.

1. Candidates  have  to  apply  ' Online '  ONLY.  The  Application  Form  other  than online mode will not be accepted.
2. Submission  of  Online  Application  Form  should  be  done  by  accessing  Diploma

Engineering  Entrance  Examination  of  Tripura  website https://deeet.tripura.gov.in/ . The Link is also provided in the DHE website: www.highereducation.tripura.gov.in. Only  one  application  is  to  be  submitted  by  a  candidate.  Duplicate  applications  may cause rejection of his/her candidature.

3. The eligibility  as  per  the  criteria  laid  down  for  Lateral  Entry  Admission  Scheme must be ensured beforehand.
4. It  is  essential  to  have  a  mobile  number  and  a  personal  email  ID  for  online application.

All future communications will be sent through the registered mobile number and email ID. CSC will not be responsible for non-receipt of any communication by the candidates if the mobile number and/or the email ID are wrong/non-existing/changed/ de-activated.

Instructions for Filling Online Application Form (Detailed guidelines for complete online application for Lateral Entry Scheme - 2021):

## a) Registration:

After accessing the DEEET Portal, the candidates have to Register themselves before applying.  A  candidate  has  to  carefully  note  down  his/her Registration  ID and Password etc. Once the registration details i.e. name, gender, date of birth and E-mail ID are entered and submitted, they cannot be changed /modified /edited under normal circumstances. Also the information must match exactly with the school admit cards, mark sheets,  certificates,  photo  identity  cards,  caste/category  certificates  etc.  which  a candidate has to produce at the time of reporting during admission.

## b) Creating Profile Details and Editing:

-  The candidates have to fill up their profile details in this section i.e. Father's name, Mother name's, Caste etc.
-  Scanned Images of Candidate's Photograph (Not more than 250 kb), Candidate's Signature (not more than100 kb) should be uploaded in JPEG / JPG format.

## c) Filling up Online Application:

Application form in online mode and noting down the system generated Application Number must be done.

## d) Uploading of  Scanned Copy of  Necessary Documents:

1. R ecent colour photograph and full Signature.
2. Marksheet of Madhyamik or equivalent examinations of CBSE / ICSE and other TBSE recognized Boards.
3. ITI final consolidated marksheet.
4. ITI pass certificate.
5. PRTC (not Citizenship certificate) as proof of residency of the applicant .
6. Caste ( SC/ST ) C ertificate of the candidate from the appropriate authority.
7. Persons  with  Disabilities ( PwD )  Certificate  issued  by  Dept.  of  Social  Welfare  / District Disabilities Rehabilitation Centres (DDRC) or competent authority approved by the State Government, if any.
8. Ward of Ex - Serviceman (ESM) Certificate (from Rajya Sainik Board/Directorate of Sainik Welfare).
9. The page of BPL ration card ( for BPL candidate ) showing Card Holder Details in

case of seeking reduced application fees.

## e) Preview of Application:

Before final submission of the application the candidates need to check and confirm the details and edit if necessary.

## f) Submitting Application:

Candidates cannot edit/ change details after final submission of the application form.

## g) Making Payment:

The  payment  of  requisite  fees  using  online  payment  gateways  should  be  done and keeping proof of the payment is a must. The fee may be paid either by credit/debit card  or  Net  Banking. No  other  modes  like  Money  Order,  DD,  IPO  etc.  will  be accepted.  No  request  for  refund  of  fees  once  remitted  by  the  candidate  will  be entertained.

## h) Printing Acknowledgement:

Candidates  need  to  take  a  Print  copy  of  the Acknowledgement  Page after  successful completion of the application. This Acknowledgement page should be submitted at the time of reporting and admission in the Institutes.

## Publication of Merit List &amp; Online Counselling:

Merit list  will  be  prepared  on  the  basis  of  total  marks  obtained  in  ITI  final  examinations  as reflected in Consolidated Statement of marks. Merit List will be published in the www.highereducation.tripura.gov.in and deeet.tripura.gov.in portal.  Subsequently,  the concerned candidates mandatorily have to make  online choice feeding before online counselling. Only after that, there will be issue of online Seat Allotment Letter .

## Online Counseling:

Before online counselling, choice feeding has to be done in the online portal by the concerned candidates.  Based  on  the  choice  feeding,  the  eligible  candidates  will  be  nominated  for admission  in  different  Polytechnic  Institute  through  online  counseling.  Available  seats  of different  courses  in  different  Polytechnic  Institute  in  state  of  Tripura  will  be  distributed amongst the eligible candidates through online counseling.
