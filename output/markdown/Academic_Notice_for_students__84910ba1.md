## ACADEMIC NOTICE

All students of 2 nd , 4 th &amp; 6 th Semester Diploma Engineering of Women's Polytechnic, Hapania  now  attending  online  classes  of  next  higher  Semester  respectively,  are  hereby directed  to  deposit  their  respective  dues  of  Government  fees  for  previous  Semester on  or before  8 th October  2020  positively to  any  branch  of Tripura  Gramin  Bank of  their convenience through prescribed 'FEE CHALLAN FORM' by downloading the same from Institute's  website 'wptripura.nic.in'. The detailed  break  -up  of  fee  structure  is  also available  in  the  said  website  of  the  Institute.  After  deposition  of  fees  'Institute's  copy'  of particular challan are to be submitted to the CASH SECTION of Women's Polytechnic by 9 th October 2020 between 12:00 noon to 3:00 p.m. of all working days. NO DRAFTS / CASH WILL BE ACCEPTED.

All  the  students  of  Diploma  Engineering  are  advised  to  maintain  strictly  social distancing and other prevailing Government guidelines to COVID -19 situations during the submission of challan copy.
