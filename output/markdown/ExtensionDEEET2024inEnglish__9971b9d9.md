## Notice for Last and Final Extension for online application of DEEET-2024 Examination

This is for general information to all concerned that, for the benefit of the  all  sections  of  the  students  of  Tripura,  the  last  date  for  online application of DEEET 2024 is hereby extended from 15 th  March 2024 to 20 th  March 2024. This is the last and final extension and after this no further  extension  for  application  of  DEEET  2024  will  be  done.  For details of the DEEET 2024 all are requested to visit the notice section of the website https://highereducation.tripura.gov.in/ .
