## Educational Notification for Spot Counselling and Diploma Engineering Admission through DEEET-2020

It  is  for  information  to  all  concerned  that Spot  Counselling and  subsequent  admission  into respective  Polytechnic  institutes  under  the  Higher  Education  Department  will  be  held  as  per  the schedule given below:

|   Sl. No. | Event                                                                                                                                      | Date                                       |
|-----------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------|
|        1. | Spot Counselling by CSC 2020 at Birchandra State Central Library, in front of West District DM Office, Agartala ( Old Secretariat Complex) | 25 th November 2020 11.00 A.M. onwards     |
|        2. | Admission in respective Polytechnic institutes after Spot Counselling under Higher Education Department.                                   | 26 th -27 th November 2020 up to 4.00 P.M. |

1. Students who have possessed merit position in DEEET 2020 common merit list and have not taken admission in any Polytechnic institutes through 1 st and 2 nd Round Counselling will be eligible for appearing at the Spot Counselling.
2. Available  vacant  of  different  categories  seats  after  1 st and  2 nd Round  admission  will  be distributed in the Spot Counselling according to merit position of DEEET 2020.
3. Students  interested  to  appear  in  the  Spot  Counselling  are  requested  to  report  CSC  at  the above mentioned venue within 10.30 am on 25 th  November 2020 ,  along with the following original document/ certificates &amp; one set of Photo copies of the certificates:-
- a) DEEET 2020  Admit  card,  b)  Madhyamik  admit  card,  c)  Madhyamik  mark  sheet,  d) PRTC, e) caste certificate etc.
4. After Spot Counselling, admission into respective polytechnic institutes will be as per the scheduled dates mentioned above.
