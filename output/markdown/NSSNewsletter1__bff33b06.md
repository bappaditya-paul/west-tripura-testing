## MASSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL'S DESK

It  gives  me  immense  pleasure  to  know  that  the  NSS  Unit  of  Women's  Polytechnic, publishing its First newsletter, with snapshots of the activities performed in last one year. This NSS newsletter of Women's Polytechnic has become a pioneering instance of best practices in the  technical  institutes  of  the  state.  I  hope  that  the  NSS  team  and  NSS  Volunteers' engaged in this novel work along with the regular academic activity ahead will work even harder to uphold the goodwill of the institute. With the support and cooperation of all the members of Women's Polytechnic family, we will definitely achieve our goal to make the institute as one of the best polytechnic in the state.                                              Dr. Tirtharaj Sen, FIE

Principal, Women's Polytechnic.
