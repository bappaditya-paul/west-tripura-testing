On  the  9 th of  November,  21  an  Essay  and  Extempore speech  competition on  Mahatma  Gandhi  &amp;  on Quit India Movement was held at WP as a part of India @ 75.

## 5. ATAL FDP on Engineering Management:

## 7.  Handover  of  the  ground  floor  of  New  Academic building:

Public  Works  Department,  Government  of  Tripura Handed over the ground floor of New Academic building to WP on 3rd November 2021

<!-- image -->

## 8.Visit of AICTE Margdarshak in WP:

Dr.  Sandeep  Grover,  Margdarshak  of Women's  Polytechnic,  Hapania  and  TTAADC  Polytechnic  Institute for  NBA Accreditation appointed by AICTE, New Delhi had a visit to the institutes on 20-21 November, 2021 and had interactions with the faculty members providing several suggestions and recommendations for a successful NBA Accreditation.

## 10. Visit of Directors on Proposed Girls Hostel:

On December 2021, the Director of Higher Education Sri N.C Sharma and the Director of OBC Welfare Smt. ## 11. Arm Forces day Collection by NSS volunteers:

The  Volunteers  of  NSS  WP  Unit  collected  a  token amount from the faculty, staff and students of the institute on Arm Forces Day and contributed the amount of Rs.700/- to the state office.

<!-- image -->

## 12. WP students won Prizes in quiz on AIDS :

The  Students  groomed  under  Dr.  Surajit  Debnath, Deptt. of MLT, received awards at the quiz competition on AIDS, organised By Tripura State AIDS Control Society in December 2021.

<!-- image -->

## 13.  Letter  from  the  Deputy  Speaker  regarding  establishing  Women's  Engineering  College  within  available infrastructure of WP:

A letter was received by the office of the Principal, WP from the Directorate of Higher Education, Government of Tripura dated 30/12/2021 with a reference to the letter from  the Deputy Speaker, Tripura Legislative  Assembly, dated 15/12/2021 for consideration  of  issues  regarding  establishment  of  Women's  Engineering  College  within  available  infrastructure  of  WP. Accordingly,  a  committee  has  been  formed  in  this  regard  involving  eminent  and  experienced  faculty  members  of  different technical institutes of Tripura including WP, Hapania, TIT, Narsingarh and NIT Agartala.
