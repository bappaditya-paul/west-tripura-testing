## Educational Notification Regarding DEEET-2020

This is for information of all concerned that online application of Diploma Engineering Entrance Examination,  Tripura  (DEEET-  2020)  will  become  live for Two  days (02) on 18 th and 19 th August 2020 . Candidates those who have not applied earlier can apply now. Students who have applied earlier need not to apply again . All other terms and conditions laid down by CSC-2020 in the first notification shall remain same.

Details about online application process are available in the website: www.highereducation.tripura.gov.in
