## Government of Tripura

Women's Polytechnic, HAPANIA

## NOTICE FOR ADMISSION THROUGH SPOT ROUND COUNSELLING IN DIPLOMA ENGINEERING-2024

Students  willing  to  take  admission  in  to  3  years  Diploma  Engineering  Courses  at  Women's  Polytechnic,  Hapania, through Spot round Counselling process (without DEEET) are directed to apply through filled-in application form (with  all  relevant  documents  as  notified  with  the  application  form)    to    the  Office  of  the  Principal,  Women's Polytechnic,  Hapania,  with  a  processing  fees  of Rs.200/.  Candidates  who  have  passed  10 th standard  or  equivalent examination from any recognised board, already appeared in DEEET 2024 examination and are Permanent resident of Tripura  are  eligible  to  apply  for  the  Spot  round  Counselling  process:  Details  of  available  seats  after  DEEET-2024 Counselling process are as follows:

-  Application form may also be downloaded from the institute website www.wptripura.nic.in .
-  All notices and merit lists will be published in the institute notice board and also in the institute website.
-  Counselling starts from 12.00 noon. Eligible candidates must report &amp; present at the institute within11.30 AM on the day of counselling. If any student fails to report on time the student may lose her merit seniority.
-  The provisional merit list will be prepared based on the sum of marks obtained in English, Mathematics &amp; Physical science/science in 10 th standard exam. Or equivalent from any recognised board. In case of tie among candidates, overall percentage of marks followed by marks obtained in Mathematics, Physical science/ science or English will be considered to break the tie.
-  Candidates who have appeared in the DEEET exam 2024 and secured rank will get merit seniority over the candidates who will apply freshly for admission.
-  ESM category  will  be  considered  if  the  application  is  forwarded  from  Rajya  Sainik  Board,  Tripura  within 28.06.2024 (4 PM).
-  In case of any dispute during the admission process, the decision of the institute will stand final.
