**Placement Cell** ........................................................................................................................................................ The Training and Placement of Women’s Polytechnic, Hapania, Tripura, works in close co-ordination with a student placement committee comprising students chosen from various departments and faculty coordinators. The T&P Cell manages training and placement activities, keeps liaison with various industrial groups and research organisations. The Training and Placement Cell of the institute centrally helps in constituting the job carrier for the students of all departments. Its excellent infrastructure supports each and every stage of the placement processes in arranging pre-placement talks, written test, group discussion, interviews etc. Placement activities of our institute consists of five pillars such as – Ø Recruitment  Ø Higher Study  Ø Entrepreneurship  Ø Internship/training  Ø Family Business  |
