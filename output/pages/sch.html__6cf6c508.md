Science & Humanities Department **  
  
 |  
| --- |  
|    
The Science & Humanities department is the basic of all the courses. It aims at developing the basic fundamentals of the students in their first year of admission to the Institute to enrich them with adequate knowledge and skills so as to enable them to adapt to the respective course of study in their second year. It is spread over different subjects which are common to all the courses. Students admitted in different branches have to study the common subjects in their first year which is divided into two semesters. Thus, it is the initial stage of the Technical Diploma Engineering Course and forms an important part of the courses.
