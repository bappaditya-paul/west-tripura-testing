About the district
West Tripura is an administrative district in the Indian state of Tripura. The district headquarters is  
Located in Agartala, which is also the state capital. As of 2012, it is the capital of Tripura.  
Most populous district (in 2018). West Tripura district is bordered by Khowai district to the north and west,  
It is bordered by Sipahijla district to the southeast and south. Tripura state has four  
After the formation of the new district, the total area of ​​the district is 983.63 sq km.
