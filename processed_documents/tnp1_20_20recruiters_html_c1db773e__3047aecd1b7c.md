| **Message from the Principal
** |
| --- |
|

| Our realization that drives us towards imparting quality education originates from the fact that teaching is a _"Mission"_ not a _"Profession"_. Our endeavour targets on producing human resources out of the women folk of the state, capable of technically surviving in the national market. I invite all to look at our track record and advise for our betterment. "Doers are not sayers and sayers are not doers." **Dr. Tirtharaj Sen, FIE** |
| **Message from the Training and Placement Officer
** |
| | On behalf of Women’s Polytechnic, Hapania, Tripura, it is my pleasure to invite you to our beautiful academic campus for conducting campus selection programmes for our students. It is my privilege to mention that this institute was established in 2002 and over the years it has earned recognition not only in Tripura but also all over the North-Eastern region. The students of this institute are occupying enviable positions in various organisations in the state and outside. Our Institute enjoys the status of best Polytechnic institute within the state in 2019. Our institute has been modernized by various State Govt. and Govt. of India funds. Faculty members of the institute are contributing significantly in research in their respective field of interest. The Institute has earned fame not only for its excellent academic and extracurricular activities but also for its research, consultancy and development activities. With this brief background I am furnishing in this web page all pertinent training & placement related information of the institute. I would request you to kindly visit our campus for selecting candidates of your choice on any date convenient to both of us with an expectation that our interaction will grow uninterruptedly over the years to come. **Dr Manash Chandra Das, TPO** |
